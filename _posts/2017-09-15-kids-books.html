--- layout: page title: Kids Books permalink: /kidsbooks/ --- I read a fair number of kids books in my line of work. Some are excellent, while others should never have been published. Here's a short record (updated sporadically).

The Good

  • Horton Hatches an Egg, by Dr. Seuss. "An elephant's faithful 100 percent", ending is biologically implausible.
  • Church mice books, by Graham Oakley. Classic British humor.
  • Salty Dog books, by Gloria and Ted Rand. Great art, and fun sailing stories.
  • Katy and the Big Snow, by Virginia Lee Burton.
  • Something Big has Been Here, by Jack Prelutsky. Fun poems with the largest vocabulary of any kids book I've ever seen.

The Bad

  • Clifford books
  • Amelia Bedelia books
