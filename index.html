Research

I am advised by Dan Roth at University of Pennsylvania, formerly in the Cognitive Computation Group at University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. My research interests are in multilingual and low-resource natural language processing. In the past, I have worked on trustworthiness (but only for situations where this handy graph is not applicable).
