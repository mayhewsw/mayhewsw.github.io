Research

I'm a machine learning engineer at Duolingo in Pittsburgh.

My PhD is from University of Pennsylvania, with Dan Roth and the Cognitive Computation Group. My thesis was on Low-Resource Named Entity Recognition. Previously, I have been at University of Illinois, Microsoft Research, MIT Lincoln Labs, and Johns Hopkins University.
